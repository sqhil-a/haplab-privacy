Privacy Policy

HapLab does not collect personal data.

HapLab is a local developer utility for testing iOS haptics and exporting small code snippets. The app does not require an account, does not track you, and does not send app usage data to a server.

Effective July 6, 2026 Version 1.0

Data Collection

HapLab does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share personal information. The app does not include analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, third-party trackers, or account sign-in.

Local App Activity

Haptic previews are generated on your device. Code export previews are generated on your device. When you tap copy, HapLab writes the selected code snippet to the system clipboard only so you can paste it elsewhere.

Network Use

HapLab does not need a network connection for its core features. It does not upload haptic selections, copied snippets, device identifiers, diagnostics, or usage history.

Support Requests

If you contact support by email, the information you choose to include is used only to respond to your request. Support emails are not used for advertising or tracking.

Children's Privacy

HapLab is a developer tool and is not directed at children. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
